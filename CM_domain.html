Content & Media

Content Work

Creating and managing written or visual material (blogs, posts, videos) to engage and inform audiences.

Benefits:

  • Professional: Improves writing, creativity, and digital marketing skills.
  • Personal: Boosts communication confidence and adaptability.

Popular Tools:

  • Writing: Google Docs, Grammarly, Hemingway Editor
  • Design: Canva, Adobe Spark
  • SEO: Ahrefs, SEMrush

Media Work

Producing and sharing content through channels like video, photography, and social media to communicate messages effectively.

Benefits:

  • Professional: Builds technical, editing, and communication skills.
  • Personal: Enhances creativity and presentation confidence.

Popular Tools:

  • Video Editing: Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, DaVinci Resolve
  • Photo Editing: Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom
  • Social Media: Buffer, Hootsuite
